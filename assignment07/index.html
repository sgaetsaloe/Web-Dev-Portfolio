Sesame's Final Project Proposal: Slice of Pi

Topic

My website is designed to help gamify learning the digits of pi. It will have the history of pi, as well as the first 100 digits as a reference,and then the user can test themselves on a seperate page to check if they have it memorized.

Audience

This website is intended for all audiences, but geared toward young people interested in mathematics and memorization. The design is a very energetic and youthful take on an often seen as boring subject

Design and Style

This website with have a bright and youthful pallet. Using mainly purple and wite to contrast and then pink as accent colors.
